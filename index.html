About SNHS

Our Mission

The Science National Honor Society recognizes outstanding achievement in science and promotes scientific literacy. We encourage students to pursue careers in science and provide opportunities for service to the school and community.

Founded on the principles of scholarship, leadership, service, and character, SNHS empowers students to make meaningful contributions to the scientific community while developing essential life skills.

To remain a member, you must fulfill all five service requirements:

1. Blood Drive Volunteer

2. School Science Fair Judge

Judge the school's science fair in December (Friday near end of December). You will be judging freshmen's science fair and posters. Required even if field trip is planned.

3. District Science Fair Judge

Judge the district science fair in January. You need your own transportation to Taylor/Hasting/Elsik High School. Event runs after school until approximately 6:00 PM.

4. Meeting Attendance

Attend all meetings unless an absence is prearranged with an officer or sponsor due to legitimate conflict.

5. Exemplify SNHS Mission

The most important requirement - exemplify the mission statement of SNHS:

"The purpose of this organization shall be to encourage participation in and recognition of scientific and intellectual thought, to advance the student's knowledge of classical and modern science, to communicate with the scientific community, to aid the civic community with its comprehension of science, and to encourage students to participate in community service and, in turn, encourage a dedication to the pursuit of scientific knowledge that benefits all mankind."
